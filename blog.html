The Beatles Blog

From Liverpool to worldwide superstardom. Formed in 1960, The Beatles transformed popular music forever. John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr combined unforgettable songwriting with groundbreaking studio innovation, becoming one of the most influential bands in history.

Throughout the 1960s, the band released a remarkable series of albums, including Rubber Soul, Revolver, Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band, The Beatles (White Album), Abbey Road, and Let It Be. Their music continues to inspire musicians and listeners around the world.

Although The Beatles officially disbanded in 1970, their legacy lives on through timeless classics such as Hey Jude, Yesterday, Come Together, Let It Be, and Here Comes the Sun. More than fifty years later, their music remains as influential and beloved as ever.
